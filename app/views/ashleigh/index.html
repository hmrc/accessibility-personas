Ashleigh

I couldn’t buy my sister the present she really wanted for her birthday because none of the fields were labelled on the website.

About Ashleigh

  • 24 year old arts graduate and administrative assistant

  • Severely sight impaired (blind)

  • Uses a screen reader

Frustrations

  • Annoyed when she has to look for a phone number or ask for help when a website doesn’t work with her screen reader.

  • Sometimes guesses what she needs to fill in because a screen reader can’t read forms if they aren’t labelled properly.

  • Can’t visualise the item she’s looking at when shopping online because there’s no description that her screen reader can understand.

Required assistive technology

Use your computer's built-in Screen reader.
